Uniform Shop

The uniform shop is operated every Tuesday and Thursday afternoon from 14:00. Mrs Brummer is responsible for the uniform shop. The shop stocks all school uniform attire for boy- and girl learners, with the exception of white school shirts. The shop also buys and sells second hand clothes that are in good condition.
